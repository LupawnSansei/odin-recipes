Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

Intsructions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
